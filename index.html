The platform

One system instead of five.

Most independent ISPs run billing in one tool, provisioning in another, monitoring in a third, and support in a fourth — none of which talk to each other. Milestone replaces the stack.

01

Provisioning & OSS

Turn up subscribers, assign speeds and IPs, and push config to your network gear automatically. New customer to live service in minutes.

02

Billing & BSS

Recurring plans, usage charges, prorated changes, autopay, and dunning. Run a billing cycle without a spreadsheet in sight.

03

Live status across every node and link, with alerts before customers notice. See an outage on the map, not in your inbox.
